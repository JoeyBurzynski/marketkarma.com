MarketReach

Search algorithms reward rich and targeted content. Taxonomies, templates, and assets in e-commerce platforms tend to be restrictive, inherently unstable, and may not fully differentiate product facets or leverage best practice markup. Escaping these constraints is critical to communicating value to the search engines and ranking effectively.

Intended as a singular solution for all aspects of organic search marketing, the publishing technology is designed around an "embrace and extend" model. MarketKarma provides best practice representation of existing content while simultaneously allowing for extension into materially different representations.

We’ve all heard that ‘content is king’. But many e-commerce platform tools for managing digital catalogs do not scale effectively. MarketKarma provides deep analysis of digital catalogs, providing algorithmic identification and segmentation of product data by feature set, demand, and topical relevance. This allows for enhancement of visibility at scale with a feature-driven virtual architecture to augment the native taxonomy.

CouponMate

CouponMate automatically finds and applies the best coupon for your purchase when you shop online. Get the latest coupons delivered automagically to your browser with CouponMate. CouponMate features coupons from over 100,000 stores, updated daily. Inspired by hours of frustration applying invalid codes, we're not just another coupon tool. Here's why:
- Searches for coupons so that you don't have to.
- Supports 100,000 stores online, updated daily.
- Automatically applies the best coupon codes to your shopping cart for selected stores with a single click
